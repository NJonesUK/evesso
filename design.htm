Base HTML templates

Use a variety of base templates to get your project started. All of these offer a lot of blocks so you can override or extend their functionality.

List of base templates

base.html
Contains most of the "container" HTML including, <head>, <body>, CSS/JS includes, and more.
base_navbar_responsive.html (Extends base.html)
Adds a responsive, fixed top navbar to the page with additional blocks for customizing the content of the navbar.
